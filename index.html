IT Project

Automatic Veggie Garden Watering System with Smart Probe Sensor

After numerous occasions of knocking on neighbours door the day before heading off on a family holiday the idea of the automatic veggie garden water system will solve all issues. The watering system will stop the need of others watering your garden or even yourself. The smart system only waters when needed as the smart probe sensors in the soil check for when water moisture levels are low and automatically tells the watering system to turn on until moisture levels return to the desired amount and the the system turns the water off. The watering system will solve the issue of your veggies dying after weeks or months of handwork just before harvest. This project is aimed at Veggie Gardens in your house or school as we want to make it as cheap as possible to set up.

There is an estimated 9.9 million houses in Australia and 9,400 schools allowing a massive demand for this product. Based off a survey conducted in 2014 which asked Australian households if they grew some sort of food in their backyard. 52% of people agreed to growing fruit. The trend for growing fruit and vegetables is increasing as 2009 24% of households grew fruit and 38% grew vegetables. But in 2014 29% grew fruit and 39% grew vegetables. There is a market need and demand for a product like an automatic watering system as this will save the the gardener time every day watering and also will save water as the exact amount of water will be used and therefor over watering will be stopped. Lastly 40% of people who don’t have veggie gardens stated they don’t have the time needed for one each day, this is where a half a days works is needed to set the system up and then it can be left until harvest but checking every few days to ensure veggies aren’t getting eaten by bugs.

For the customer they will only need to supply a tap and a water supply, power and an irrigation system. The automatic watering system will connect between the tap and the irrigation system and act as a barrier to stop the flow of water and the smart probe sensors will go into the soil to test the moisture in the soil. The design for the box will be a square. will a waterproof screen and buttons as inputs to change the days and times of which the water will go through the irrigation system. The box itself will be black in colour and have mounting holes and an IP 66 rating, making this box water proof to and protected to 12.5 mm nozzle at 100LPM at 3 meters away and dust proof. Each corner of the box will have little holes where the purchaser has the option to wall mount the box. Ideally the box is to be off the ground to limit the amount of water around it. The size of the box will be as small as possible to allow to not take up too much space. The box will be made out of hard plastic, to help to deal with damage and make it durable for a long lasting life. The box will be available in either grey, black or white. These colours allow for different scenarios in which what colour fits in to each garden better. The purpose for this system is to allow the gardener to have as little interaction with the watering of his veggie garden as the smart probe sensors do the hard work for them and detect when the garden needs to be watered. Also the gardener has the option to set multiple times throughout the day as you might like your veggie garden to be watered both day and night. The length of time the the irrigation system runs for can be set to the exact time. When setting your preference into each day they time of which they are set too can change day to day and allow for different times on all seven days. The smart probe sensors also detect when to turn off the irrigation system as this will allow for no more over watering of the garden and lead to saving more water.On the face of the box there will be a six buttons, four of which are used to control and change the settings, including up, down, left and right. There will be a central button within the arrows which will be used a the enter button to confirm the changes and preferences set. The last button will be an on and off switch, which will allow for the system to be turned off in periods throughout the year when it’s not being used to save power. Within the display it will show how much water has been used for the day and for the week and weekly rests. this is too ensure you are getting the correct amount of water each time and not too much or not enough. This feature will allow for better water saving. As this automatic water system is just the box with controls this can be used with any irrigation system at your household. Making this product perfect for every home.

The software within the system will be need to be made by a manufacturer. The program within the hardware will also be needed to be outsourced to a professional. I have not much knowledge within the software and hardware field in this project, but finding the resources required to build the project will not be too hard as there is not many major components. Creating the structural features such as the box where the hardware and software will be kept can be made by the individual but as we sell the product complete this can be made in a factory which is out sourced to a company that provides this service.

Basic tool and gardening knowledge to set up your own irrigation system within your veggie garden. Also having running water and power at the garden so that our smart water system will work. The system will be set up to be easy to use and basically plug and play. All that will be needed to do is to place the smart sensor probes in the desired places into the soil so that they can sense the moisture within the soil. It will be feasible to find the skills required to create my product as it is simple to create as not much is needed for the system to work besides allowing water to pass through and stop according to the sensors.

If my projects was to be successful this would solve the issue of time of needing to monitor your veggie garden everyday. This will allow more Australians to grow their own fruit and vegetables at anytime of the year without the hassle of watering them morning and night. This will allow more free time for the gardener to spend doing other activities they enjoy while still getting the goodness of home grown fruit and vegetables. There will be no need to ask your friends or neighbours to come and water your garden while you're away, you can go away and feel comfortable that you garden is getting the adequate water it needs.
